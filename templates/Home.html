Project Information

- In this project, I relied only on machine learning, not deep learning, using a support vector machine algorithm that achieved a very high score of 99%.

- I have used in this project 1000 images (average 200 images for each character), and after the operations and processing of those images became 382 images.

- I also relied in the project on opencv haar cascades, and wave analysis by Fourier transform.


How to use

- Upload the image by clicking on choose file and it must be in the following formats [png - jpg - jpeg] and then click on upload.


Important Notes

- You must choose a picture in which the face is completely clear and not a picture of the side of the face or part of it.

- You must upload the latest photo of the person you want to get to know (not when they were young).

- When you enter a picture that contains more than one person, for example (Pedri with Messi) it will only show the result of the highest match between them, not the two.

- If you enter a photo of a person outside of the people in the project, a result will appear that more matches the person whose photo you have entered with the people present,

  for example (if you enter a photo of someone who looks like Messi, the result will show that it is Messi and so on).

- In the event that the photo you entered for someone did not match the people present, a random result will appear to you.
