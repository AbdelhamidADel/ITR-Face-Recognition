France Football Celebration 2021

The 2021 Ballon d'Or was the 65th annual ceremony of the Ballon d'Or , presented by France Football, and recognising the best footballers in the world in 2021. The nominees for the ceremony were announced on 8 October 2021. Right before the ceremony, France Football announced that along with the Men's and Women's Ballon d'Or, Kopa Trophy and Yashin Trophy, two new awards would be given: the Best Club and Best Striker of the Year.
